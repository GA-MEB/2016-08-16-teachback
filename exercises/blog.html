The Best Batman -- Bale or Affleck?

Joker: Neither. The correct answer is Kevin Conroy.

Of course. As far as the best live action Batman, however, that's another question. Here are the pros and cons of Christian Bale's and Ben Affleck's portrayals of the caped crusader in Christopher Nolan's Dark Knight trilogy and the new DC cinematic universe films, respectively.

    Christian Bale
  • Pro: Right level of physicality.
  • Pro: Great portrayal of the man behind the mask, Bruce Wayne.
  • Con: "WHERE RR THUH HSSTEGES"
    Ben Affleck
  • Pro: Affleck's Batman had the feel of a strategist and planner -- something he's known for, but something Bale's Batman just... wasn't.
  • Pro: Strong performance both as Bruce Wayne and as Batman.
  • Con: Writers, and a desparate, desparate studio that jus can't keep its mitts out of anything .
